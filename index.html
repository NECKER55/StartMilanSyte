frequently asked questions

My background is not in business, can I still join?add

Of course, our members come from a variety of fields, not just from business backgrounds.

Do I have to have a startup to join?add

No. You should have an intrinsic motivation towards entrepreneurship topics, a hands on mentality and willingness to learn.

I’m not a student in MILAN, can I still join START MILAN?add

No, to become a member, you need to be a student enrolled in one of the universities in Milan. You are, however, very welcome to join one of our public events or collaborate with us.

How active is the community?add

We prioritize an active and lively community by organizing over 20 events and community meetups, as well as working in smaller groups on various projects.

How can I benefit from joining START MILAN?add

You will get immersed into the entrepreneurship ecosystem, and find a community of like-minded, highly motivated and inspiring students that quickly become good friends. We have frequent event formats where you will gain new skills and insights and tackle hands-on challenges. As an active member you will join one of our departments, where you have additional opportunities to gain new skills and carry out projects.

What are the tasks I have to do as a member?add

You will choose one of our departments to become an active member and take on different projects. By doing this, you will have a chance to practice proactive project management and execution, and develop new department specific hard skills like building automations, designing a marketing campaign, partner acquisition, event organization or recruiting of new members.

Ready to join our community?

Apply to join our community and gain access to funding, mentorship, and a global network of innovators.
