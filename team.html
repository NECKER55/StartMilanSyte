OUR DEPARTMENTS

👥

PEOPLE

We find the raw diamonds in Milan's startup ecosystem, work hard with you to take the next step in your entrepreneurial journey, and most importantly, we build a community that feels like a family!

📈

GROWTH

We find creative and fun ways to engage with our ecosystem, communicate the values, vision, and happenings in START Milan, and connect to stakeholders, like students, industry partners, universities and other initiatives.

📅

EVENTS

Our events are the key to a strong community and an active network. By organizing diverse events we tailor to the interests of our members, thus provide an opportunity to gain new knowledge, skills and experiences that promote growth and innovation.

⚙️

OPERATIONS

The Operations department is the backbone of our organization. We make sure our processes run smoothly and that START Milan is set up for long-term success financially, legally and operationally.

🤝

PARTNERSHIPS

We establish and nurture flourishing partnerships that unite entrepreneurial minds. We work toward a positive impact for our members, partners and Milan's entrepreneurship ecosystem.
